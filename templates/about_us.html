About Us

Building the future of open link management.

Our Mission

LinkShort was built with a simple goal: to provide enterprise-grade link management tools that are free, fast, and accessible to everyone. No signups, no paywalls, no hidden fees.

What We Offer

We offer a suite of professional tools designed for marketers, creators, and businesses:

  • Bulk URL Shortener: Shorten hundreds of links at once
  • QR Code Designer: Create custom QR codes with your brand colors
  • UTM Builder: Generate trackable campaign links
  • A/B Link Testing: Test multiple link variants

Why Free?

We believe essential marketing tools should be accessible to everyone — from solo creators to large agencies. Our platform is supported by non-intrusive advertising, allowing us to keep all features free.

Our Values

  • Transparency: No tracking beyond what's necessary for functionality
  • Speed: Optimized for instant results
  • Simplicity: Clean, intuitive interfaces
  • Privacy: Your data stays yours
