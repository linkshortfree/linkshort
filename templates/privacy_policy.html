Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

1. Introduction

LinkShort ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • URLs you submit for shortening
  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our tools and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Third-party advertisers (Google AdSense) may also use cookies to display personalized ads.

5. Data Retention

Shortened URLs are stored indefinitely to ensure redirects continue to work. You may request deletion of specific links by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google's use of cookies is governed by their own privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at support@linkshort.live for any requests.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@linkshort.live.
