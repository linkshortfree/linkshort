Our Story

LinkShort was born out of a simple frustration: the digital world is cluttered with tools that are either too expensive or too intrusive. We wanted to build something different—a platform that is powerful, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To provide enterprise-grade URL shortening and QR code tools for free, helping individuals and businesses build trust with their audience through clean, branded links.

Our Vision

A web where links are not just destinations, but transparent signals of quality and security. We aim to be the gold standard for bulk link management.

Why Transparency Matters

In an age of phishing and digital mistrust, we believe in radical transparency. That's why we focus on custom aliases—giving you the power to tell the world exactly where your links are leading. We don't hide behind random strings; we put your brand front and center.

The Team Behind the Tool

We are a small, dedicated group of developers and marketers who believe in the power of the open web. LinkShort is our way of giving back, providing the tools we wish we had when we were starting our digital journeys.
