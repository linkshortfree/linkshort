Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool completely free?

Yes! LinkShort is designed to be a free utility for everyone. There are no paywalls for bulk processing or QR generation.

How do I use the bulk feature?

Simply upload a CSV or Excel file where the first column contains your long URLs. You can also paste a list of URLs directly into the text box (one per line). Our system will process them all instantly.

Can I customize the QR codes?

Absolutely. You can change the foreground color, background color, size, and even add a greeting text (like "Scan Me") to the top of the QR code.
