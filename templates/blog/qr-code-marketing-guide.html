The Ultimate Guide to QR Code Marketing in 2026

QR codes have made a massive comeback, becoming an essential bridge between the offline and online worlds. From restaurant menus to interactive billboard ads, they are everywhere.

Why QR Codes Work

The frictionless nature of scanning a code makes it incredibly easy for users to access information, make payments, or join mailing lists. In 2026, the key is not just having a QR code, but having a *branded* one.

The Power of Custom Greetings

At LinkShort, we allow you to add custom greetings and emojis to your QR codes. This simple addition makes the code feel more personal and less like a sterile diagnostic tool. It welcomes the user even before they land on your page.

Best Practices for Print

  • Contrast: Ensure there's high contrast between the code and the background.
  • Size: Don't make it too small; ensure it's easily scannable from a distance.
  • Clear Call-to-Action: Always tell the user *why* they should scan.
